Always a new story

Corporate Video

Welcome to the journey of Itaca, where every surface tells a story. Itaca began in 2009 with a clear idea: to make special and beautiful surfaces for people who like luxury. They also wanted to show the value of craftsmanship and the beauty it brings. Itaca brings Italian designs to the inside of homes in India.

History

2009

A Journey Through Time, Since 2009. From its humble beginnings to a thriving legacy, Itaca has been shaping history for over a decade, one chapter at a time. Join us on this remarkable voyage through the past, as we continue to pave the way for a bright future.

2013

Itaca expanded its horizons by venturing into the world of wall tiles, marking a significant milestone in our journey. With innovation and quality as our guiding principles, we've been adding a touch of elegance to your spaces ever since.

2018

A new chapter of innovation and excellence began for Itaca as we delved into GVT production. Our commitment to quality and aesthetics has been redefining spaces and leaving a lasting impression. Join us as we continue to set the standard in GVT tiles.

2022

We introduced a new era of design possibilities with the launch of Slab Tiles at Itaca. Elevating interiors to the next level, our commitment to innovation remains unwavering. Explore a world of endless design potential with Itaca Slab Tiles.

2023

we opened Itaca's GVT Unit 2. This shows how we always aim to do our best and keep getting better. We're bringing more new and great ideas, top quality, and better service to the world of Glazed Vitrified Tiles. Join us as we reach even higher levels in this tile business!

2025

In 2025, Itaca took a meaningful step forward by welcoming a new company into its fold. This strategic move strengthens our foundation and opens new avenues for collaboration and growth. With shared values and a unified vision, we continue shaping the future of design and innovation.
