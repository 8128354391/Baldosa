At the heart of Russia's evolving architectural landscape, we are not just tile makers — we are storytellers in stone and glaze. Every tile we craft captures the spirit of design, blending timeless traditions with bold, modern expression.
ABOUT US

Born from a passion for texture, color, and the quiet strength of ceramic, our collections are inspired by the natural beauty of Russia's landscapes — from the icy blues of the north to the rich earth tones of the steppes. Each piece is more than a surface; it's a brushstroke in the hands of architects, designers, and dreamers.

We believe in tiles that do more than cover walls — they elevate spaces, spark emotion, and stand the test of time. With cutting-edge technology and artistic integrity, we transform everyday ceramics into statements of style.
